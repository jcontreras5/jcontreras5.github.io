Fashion-Love Shadow

Well, what can I say about this song. The intro rips something straight out of a spaceship. Sit back and listen to those sweet twangs from that scratchy nylon guitar. Space drums right from Neptune. Are you living in the love shadow?.

Benjamin Diamond- Slowly Diamond

Benjamin Diamond is one of those artist that makes you feel awesome simply by his sound. The song sounds something that Daft Punk might have made or any of the pioneers of the French House movement. We are going back to Studio 54 for this one. Do you remember?

I-Level- Minefield

With the lyrics reminiscent of Men at Work, I-Level comes in this one only imited promo record with their song Minefield. Those drums, man.Just listen to the bongos.Why would anyone want to dance in a minefield? Who knows , but who cares as long as you are doing it to I-Level.

Todd Terje- Delorean Dynamite

If there were to be music playing in the Dolorean in Back to the Future movies while Marty Mcfly and Doc Brown are traveling through time, This would be the song that would be playing. There is nothing else to be said about the song except brace yourself for chills and goosebumps ase you cruise down the highway to this one.

Friendly Fires-Paris(Aeroplane Remix)

Aeroplanes the name. This cover is actually better than the original and puts a balearic spin on an alternative rock song. The edit is a little bit long but it is meant to be enjoyed while the sun is falling on the beach in Ibiza.One day maybe you will also live in Paris. Who knows, you know?
